<h2 style="color: #ff7043; border-bottom: 2px solid #d84315; padding-bottom: 5px; font-size: 2em; margin-top: 30px;">Commands</h2>

<h3 style="color: #ffab91; margin-top: 25px; font-size: 1.5em; border-bottom: 1px solid #3e2723; padding-bottom: 3px;">/backwoodstime on</h3>
<p>Enables debug mode for the player. While active, the current <code style="color: #e06c75; background: #282c34; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px;">backwoods_time</code> value is displayed above the hotbar in real time while inside [[dimensions|The Backwoods dimension]], and the current <code style="color: #e06c75; background: #282c34; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px;">void_time</code> value is displayed while inside [[dimensions|The Loss dimension]].</p>

<h3 style="color: #ffab91; margin-top: 25px; font-size: 1.5em; border-bottom: 1px solid #3e2723; padding-bottom: 3px;">/backwoodstime off</h3>
<p>Disables debug mode and hides the debug display.</p>

<h3 style="color: #ffab91; margin-top: 25px; font-size: 1.5em; border-bottom: 1px solid #3e2723; padding-bottom: 3px;">/setbackwoodstime [value]</h3>
<p>Sets the player's <code style="color: #e06c75; background: #282c34; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px;">backwoods_time</code> directly to the specified value. Useful for testing specific degradation stages without waiting.</p>
<table class="article-table" style="width: 300px; font-size: 0.9em; border-collapse: collapse; background: rgba(20, 15, 15, 0.4); margin-top: 10px;">
    <tr style="background: #3e2723; color: #fff;"><th style="padding: 6px 8px;">Stage</th><th style="text-align: right; padding: 6px 8px;">Threshold Value</th></tr>
    <tr style="border-bottom: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1);"><td style="padding: 6px 8px;">Stage One</td><td style="text-align: right; padding: 6px 8px;">6,000</td></tr>
    <tr style="border-bottom: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1);"><td style="padding: 6px 8px;">Stage Two</td><td style="text-align: right; padding: 6px 8px;">9,600</td></tr>
    <tr style="border-bottom: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1);"><td style="padding: 6px 8px;">Stage Three</td><td style="text-align: right; padding: 6px 8px;">16,800</td></tr>
    <tr><td style="padding: 6px 8px;">Stage Four</td><td style="text-align: right; padding: 6px 8px;">24,000</td></tr>
</table>

<h3 style="color: #ffab91; margin-top: 25px; font-size: 1.5em; border-bottom: 1px solid #3e2723; padding-bottom: 3px;">/setvoidtime [value]</h3>
<p>Sets the player's <code style="color: #e06c75; background: #282c34; padding: 2px 4px; border-radius: 4px;">void_time</code> directly to the specified value. Used for testing behavior inside [[dimensions|The Loss dimension]]. At a value of '''12,000 or above''', Plaque blocks begin spreading faster following the bell resonate sound.</p>

<h3 style="color: #ffab91; margin-top: 25px; font-size: 1.5em; border-bottom: 1px solid #3e2723; padding-bottom: 3px;">/rotstate [target] totem [on | off | infinity on | infinity off]</h3>
<p>Directly toggles the active state of the Rot sentinel's totem mode. While active, the Rot gains amplified shockwaves, larger blast radii, increased damage, and more relentless combat behaviors. Setting it to <code style="color: #e06c75;">infinity on</code> unlocks its absolute limit state, complete with massive passive health regeneration.</p>

<h3 style="color: #ffab91; margin-top: 25px; font-size: 1.5em; border-bottom: 1px solid #3e2723; padding-bottom: 3px;">/combatkit [backwoods | fractus | rot]</h3>
<p>A unified command providing specialized developer combat loadouts with an interactive selection window. Using this command equips the executing player based on the chosen parameter:</p>

<h4 style="color: #ffccbc; margin-top: 20px; font-size: 1.25em;">fractus</h4>
<p>Prepares the player's environment and stats specifically for intense combat encounters against Fractus entities. Executing this triggers the following adjustments:</p>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc; padding-left: 20px; margin-top: 5px;">
    <li>Grants infinite <code style="color: #e06c75;">Regeneration X</code>, <code style="color: #e06c75;">Speed X</code>, <code style="color: #e06c75;">Resistance X</code>, and infinite <code style="color: #e06c75;">Saturation</code> status effects.</li>
    <li>Modifies player attributes directly, boosting base <code style="color: #e06c75;">max_health</code> to <strong>200 (100 Hearts)</strong> and step height to <strong>3 blocks</strong>.</li>
    <li>Gives 1x standard Netherite Sword and 1x standard Bow.</li>
    <li>Gives 128x Tipped Arrows of Strong Harming.</li>
    <li>Supplies 64x Fractus Spawn Eggs and 64x Fractus Prime Spawn Eggs for manual entity population.</li>
</ul>

<h4 style="color: #ffccbc; margin-top: 20px; font-size: 1.25em;">backwoods</h4>
<p>Equips a fully maximized endgame survival loadout tailored for enduring high-threat environments and testing base combat viability within the dimension:</p>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc; padding-left: 20px; margin-top: 5px;">
    <li><strong>Armor Suite:</strong> Equips a full set of maxed Netherite Armor directly onto the player:
        <ul style="list-style-type: circle; padding-left: 20px;">
            <li><code style="color: #c8e6c9;">Helmet:</code> Protection IV, Unbreaking III, Mending, Aqua Affinity, Respiration IV</li>
            <li><code style="color: #c8e6c9;">Chestplate:</code> Protection IV, Unbreaking III, Mending, Thorns IV</li>
            <li><code style="color: #c8e6c9;">Leggings:</code> Protection IV, Unbreaking III, Mending, Swift Sneak III</li>
            <li><code style="color: #c8e6c9;">Boots:</code> Protection IV, Unbreaking III, Mending, Feather Falling IV, Depth Strider III</li>
        </ul>
    </li>
    <li><strong>Weapons & Tactics:</strong> Gives 1x Netherite Axe (Sharpness V, Efficiency V, Unbreaking III, Mending), 1x Bow (Power V, Punch II, Unbreaking III), and equips 1x Shield (Unbreaking III, Mending) into the off-hand.</li>
    <li><strong>Logistics Support:</strong> Automatically drops 128x Tipped Arrows of Strong Harming, 128x Oak Planks, 128x Torches, 64x Enchanted Golden Apples, and 2x Backwoods portal catalysts directly into the inventory.</li>
</ul>

<h4 style="color: #ffccbc; margin-top: 20px; font-size: 1.25em;">rot</h4>
<p>Provides a specialized endgame combat loadout tailored for encounters with the sentinel, mirroring the Backwoods kit with extreme survivability supplements:</p>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc; padding-left: 20px; margin-top: 5px;">
    <li><strong>Armor Suite:</strong> Equips a full set of maxed Netherite Armor directly onto the player identical to the Backwoods suite.</li>
    <li><strong>Weapons & Tactics:</strong> Provides a maxed Netherite Axe, a maxed Bow, and a maxed Shield equipped into the off-hand.</li>
    <li><strong>Logistics Support:</strong> Drops 128x Tipped Arrows of Strong Harming, 64x Enchanted Golden Apples, and <strong>12x Totems of Undying</strong> into the inventory.</li>
    <li><strong>TACZ Integration:</strong> If the <em>Timeless and Classics Zero (TaCZ)</em> mod is loaded, automatically grants a Minigun set to AUTO fire mode and an infinite Creative Ammo Box.</li>
</ul>
