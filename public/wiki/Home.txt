<!-- WELCOME INTRO -->
<div style="background: #111; border: 1px solid #3c1414; padding: 25px; margin-bottom: 35px; box-shadow: inset 0 0 10px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.8);">
<p style="font-size: 1.1em; line-height: 1.8; margin: 0; color: #dcd6cd; text-align: justify;">
'''Backwoods''' is a mod that adds new horror dimensions, hostile entities with unique behaviors, progression items, structures, and atmospheric blocks. It also introduces a mental degradation system that changes how you survive the longer you remain in the unknown.
</p>
<p style="font-size: 1.05em; line-height: 1.8; margin: 20px 0 0 0; color: #8e8375; font-style: italic; border-left: 2px solid #5c1414; padding-left: 18px; text-align: justify;">
The mod aims to diversify and intensify survival through oppressive exploration, limited safety, and enemies that require awareness and timing rather than simple combat. With Backwoods, you will once again feel fear while traveling, looting, and descending into dark places, because danger is no longer just around you, it is watching you.
</p>
</div>
