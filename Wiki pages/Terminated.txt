<h3 style="color: #c4bfa5; margin-top: 40px; margin-bottom: 12px; font-size: 1.15em; letter-spacing: 0.05em; text-transform: uppercase; font-weight: 500;">Azazel Boss Reference</h3>
<p style="margin-bottom: 15px; font-size: 0.95em;">Azazel is the primary boss of the <strong>Cult of Azazel</strong> mod. The boss fight exists in two distinct forms: the Divine Chariot Azazel and Azazel, The Awakened. Fought with multiple state transitions, shields, prisons, and fire rings, Azazel was completely neutralized and cataloged during Cycle 80.</p>

<h4 style="color: #eaeaea; margin-top: 25px; margin-bottom: 10px; font-size: 1.1em; font-weight: 500;">Active Attack Profiles</h4>
<p style="margin-bottom: 15px; font-size: 0.95em;">The combat cycle executes diverse behavioral states:</p>
<ul style="margin: 0 0 20px 0; padding-left: 18px; list-style-type: square; line-height: 1.7; font-size: 0.9em; color: #9c9788;">
    <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>Aggro Trigger:</strong> Screams, shows a full-screen title <code>Mistake</code>, and triggers Bad Omen detection.</li>
    <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>Wind Attack (State 1):</strong> Deals <code>5.0</code> damage and blows back targets within a 20-block radius.</li>
    <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>Defense Shield (State 2):</strong> Becomes invulnerable for 5 seconds after receiving <code>5-20</code> hits, healing 20.0 HP.</li>
    <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>Wheel Attack (State 3):</strong> Rapid dashing dashes, dealing <code>4.0</code> contact damage per hit.</li>
    <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>Arrow Attack (State 4):</strong> Invokes one of three randomly chosen modes: a 30-arrow rain (Variant 0), a 20 Evoker Fang wave (Variant 1), or a 10 laser beam ring (Variant 2).</li>
    <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>Pray / Buff Minions (State 5):</strong> Buffs nearby <code>BelieverEntity</code> minions with 7.5 seconds of invulnerability.</li>
    <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>Pull &amp; Launch (State 11 &amp; 12):</strong> Siphons players within 20 blocks before launching them vertically or detonating a 5-block explosion dealing <code>8.0</code> damage.</li>
    <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>Midas Ring of Fire (State 13):</strong> Summons Guardians or Midas units, creates an expanding ring of fire dealing <code>8.0</code> tick damage, and turns random inventory items into Raw Gold if targets stand within 6 blocks for 10 seconds.</li>
    <li style="margin-bottom: 0;"><strong>Blackstone Prison (State 14):</strong> Entombment columns (5 blocks radius, 6 blocks height). Grows 5 blocks taller dynamically using Grand Door Parts if targets fly or leap over the barrier.</li>
</ul>

<h4 style="color: #eaeaea; margin-top: 25px; margin-bottom: 10px; font-size: 1.1em; font-weight: 500;">The Sparing Phase (Mercy Cycle)</h4>
<p style="margin-bottom: 15px; font-size: 0.95em;">Upon reaching <strong>5% Health or lower</strong>, Azazel ceases all aggressive behavior and initiates a 14-second clock ticking countdown:</p>
<ul style="margin: 0 0 20px 0; padding-left: 18px; list-style-type: square; line-height: 1.7; font-size: 0.9em; color: #9c9788;">
    <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>Spare Condition:</strong> Right-clicking Azazel with an Iron, Diamond, or Netherite Sword or Axe consumes the weapon, causing the boss to depart in peace.</li>
    <li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>Refusal Cooldown:</strong> If 14 seconds pass without interaction, the boss re-aggroes at full combat effectiveness.</li>
    <li style="margin-bottom: 0;"><strong>Execution Drops:</strong> Striking Azazel during the countdown prompts a full death explosion, dropping the primary <strong>Loot Barrel</strong>.</li>
</ul>
